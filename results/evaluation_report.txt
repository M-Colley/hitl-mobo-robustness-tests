Recommended primary evaluation
- Report BOTH excess regret (robustness) and absolute noisy-run regret
  (deployment quality): excess regret alone can crown acquisitions that
  are uniformly bad in baseline and noisy conditions alike.
- Lower excess AUC simple regret is better (within one condition).
- For ANY comparison across jitter onsets use the post-onset,
  per-noisy-iteration excess AUC; whole-run AUC scales with the window.
- Inference regret (the *_inference_* metrics) scores the design the
  experimenter would actually pick from noisy observations; it is the
  deployment-relevant recommendation quality.
- Response-step excess L2 is descriptive only.
